Final Prototype Design And Implementation

Motivation

College students have been disregarding their emotional and physical health due to unrealistic productivity expectations set by themselves and others. In response to it, our group decided to create a tangible stress ball which the will help the users relax and destress through meditation. The user can squeeze the stress ball and recieve feedback and the device can also be used as guide to a meditation practice. Medidate Me, the tangible stess ball is small and portable which allow the user to use at anytime and any location.
